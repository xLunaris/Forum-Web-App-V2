About EDU Connect

EDU-Connect is a web-based platform designed to enhance communication between educators and students.

The platform provides an easy-to-use interface for lecturers or educators to post course/subject feedback collection posts, which students can then respond to.

EDU-Connect also features a functionality that automatically classifies the sentiments of the feedbacks, allowing educators to quickly understand the overall sentiment of their students.

Additionally, the platform provides a platform for students to create discussion posts, where they can discuss anything related to their studies with their peers.

EDU-Connect is a valuable tool for educators to gain insights into their teaching and for students to have a voice in their education.
